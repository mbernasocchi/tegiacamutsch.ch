The space
Situated in the heart of the ski and outdoor destination LAAX, the Tegia Camutsch is your ideal getaway in the mountain.
With up to six beds, a fully equipped kitchen, a roomy living room with fireplace, a private garden and a terrace and even an office desk, the Tegia Camutsch is perfectly suited to be your home away from home.
Guest access
You will have access to the whole right half of the house, the shared laundry room and the ski and boot dries.
Guest interaction
We, Marco and Eliane, live in the other half of the Tegia Camutsch, so if you have any questions please come and see us :)
Neighborhood overview
Very quiet and sunny location.
Getting around
You can reach the lake, shops, restaurants and public transportation (and local shuttle) in less than 5 minutes walking.
In winter a local shuttle to the ski resorts leaves 2 minutes walking from the house.
